### 5 Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is this actually free?**
Yes. SubTract is a free tool built by the team at Credex. There is no paywall to see your audit results. 

**2. Why do you need my team size?**
Our audit engine calculates "Ghost Seats." If you have 10 engineers but are paying for 15 Copilot licenses, we flag that discrepancy instantly. 

**3. Do you connect to our corporate cards?**
No. SubTract is completely manual and secure. We do not ask for OAuth permissions, bank connections, or API keys. 

**4. How accurate is the pricing data?**
Extremely. We manually verify the official pricing tiers of Cursor, Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Windsurf, and Gemini every month.

**5. How does Credex make money?**
If your audit reveals significant savings (>$500/month), you have the option to hire Credex to automatically negotiate and capture those vendor discounts for you. We only make money if we save you money.
